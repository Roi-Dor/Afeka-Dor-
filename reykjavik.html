Reykjavik, Iceland

A colorful northern capital

Reykjavik, the capital of Iceland, is a small yet charming city known for its colorful houses, scenic harbor, and the iconic Hallgrímskirkja church. It has a calm, cool atmosphere with beautiful natural views all around.

From the city, you can easily reach Iceland’s dramatic landscapes: glaciers, geysers, waterfalls, and — with luck — the northern lights glowing in the winter sky.
