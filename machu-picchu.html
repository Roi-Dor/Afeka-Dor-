Machu Picchu, Peru

An ancient Inca city hidden in the mountains

Machu Picchu is an ancient Inca settlement perched high in the Andes Mountains. It overlooks deep valleys and is often surrounded by drifting clouds, giving the entire place a mystical atmosphere.

The terraced fields, stone structures, and breathtaking mountain views make it feel like stepping back in time. It’s one of those rare places that looks exactly like a postcard — only the real thing is even more impressive.
