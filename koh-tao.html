Koh Tao, Thailand

A small island with crystal-clear water

Koh Tao is a small island in the Gulf of Thailand known for its quiet beaches and turquoise water. It’s one of the most popular places in the world for diving and snorkeling thanks to its coral reefs and colorful marine life.

The island has a relaxed vibe, with beach bars, quiet bays, and viewpoints that overlook the entire coastline — especially beautiful at sunset.
